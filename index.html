How it works

Three steps to self-mastery

Soulio fits into your routine in under a minute a day.

01

Log your mood

Open Soulio daily and do a quick 30-second check-in. Select how you feel, pick context tags like "work", "sport" or "family", and add a brief journal note if you wish.

02

Talk to Soulio

Your AI coach reads your entire history and responds with empathy and insight. Ask it anything "why am I stressed?" or "what makes me happy?" and get personalised answers.

03

Discover your patterns

Over days and weeks, analytics reveal the hidden rhythms of your emotional life what lifts you up, what drags you down, and how to optimise your wellbeing.
